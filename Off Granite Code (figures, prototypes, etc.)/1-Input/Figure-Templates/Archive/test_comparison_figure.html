Analyzing the Time Component is Critical: Though it is important to look for disparities in overall utilization, such an analysis alone will fail to identify disparities in the temporal aspect of care delivery.
Temporally Aware Measures Exhibit Convergent Validity: Time aware measures tend to agree with each other, suggesting that they are capturing similar constructs. However, they vary in what, specifically, they measure as well as their sensitivity, thus the choice of measure should be based on the research question.
